Kale, Chilli & Feta Pasta

Pastas don't have to be drenched in heavy sauces. Here, kale, chilli flakes and garlic are stir-fried till soft and fragrant, then tossed with bow-tie pasta, chopped cherry tomatoes and creamy feta. With a little cooking water to bring it all together, the result is a wonderfully fresh vegetarian meal.

Jacket Sweet Potatoes

Australia Day cals for the classics, like crispy-skinned jacket potatoes with fork-tender flesh. We've used sweet potato for a healthy, low GI twist, and filled it with a mexican-inspired corn and coriander salsa, gooey melted cheddar and lime-spiked yoghurt sauce. Cooked in the oven or barbecue, this vego meal is perfect for young and old alike.

Roasted Vegetable Grain Bowl

Let the oven do the work for you with this bumper summer grain bowl. Just peel and chop some sweet potato, capsicum and red onion, then roast until tender and caramelised, while nutty freekah cooks on the stovetop. Add toastedalmonds and golden pan-fried haloumi, toss, then enjoy!
